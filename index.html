How To Play

Control Panel

  • Healthy Cells - Number of healthy cells at the start of the imulation
  • Cancer Cells - Number of cancer cells at the start of the imulation
  • Cell Types -
    • Treatment Sensitive - Populate the cells with treatment sensitive cancer cells included
    • Chemo Resistant - Populate the cells with chemo resistant cancer cells included
    • Radiation Resistant - Populate the cells with radiation resistant cancer cells included
  • Intensity - The percentage of cells that are destroyed every cycle. For example, an intensity of 50% would randomly destroy 50% of all cells whenever a treatment is applied
  • Name - Enter your name to save your high scores. If no name is provided, a random name is generated for you
  • Reset High Scores - Reset the high score board

Health & Tumor Bar

  • Health - The number of healthy cells of the patient. The game is over when this health reaches 0. Maximum health is 10000
  • Tumor Size - The number of tumor cells of the patient. The game is over when this bar is full

High Score Board

  • High Scores - A list of the players with the highest patient survival time (measured in cycles)

Console

  • Display -
    • Cell Counts - Displays information about the different cell types in the simulation
    • Current Cycle - The current cycle in the sim. The cycle increases every 5 seconds. Every cycle, each cell in the sim divides into two, therefore the total number of cells are doubled.
  • Buttons-
    • Play - Start the simulation
    • Chemo - Apply chemotherapy treatment to the patient
    • Radiation - Apply radiation therapy treatment to the patient
    • Reset - Repopulate the cells with new cell types

Patient

  • Cells - This displays the patient and a graphical representation of the cells in the affected area of the patient's body.

Have fun!
